How It Developed

3 Easy Steps

Data Collection

Comprehensive custom datasets were created containing a vast array of pregnancy-related information, including medical guidelines, developmental milestones, and expert advice.

AI Training

The AI model was trained using Rasa AI. The training process involved inputting the custom datasets into the model to enable it to generate appropriate responses.The chatbot was developed within the Anaconda environment, utilizing Python and Rasa frameworks.

Continuous Improvement

We constantly update our knowledge base to provide the latest information.Extensive testing was conducted to ensure the accuracy and reliability of responses.
